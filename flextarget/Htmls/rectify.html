Rectification ensures accurate bullet registration. Without it, bullets won't be captured properly.

  • Camera Setup: Stabilize your phone with a tripod and ensure all 4 QR codes are visible. Tap to focus or pinch to zoom if needed.
  • Auto Rectification: Wait for the beep when all 4 QR codes are recognized. The target perspective will then be corrected.
  • Note:If you move the phone, repeat the process.
