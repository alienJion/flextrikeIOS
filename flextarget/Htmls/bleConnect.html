Enable Bluetooth

Ensure Bluetooth is enabled on your device before attempting to connect. Navigate to your device settings and turn on Bluetooth functionality. This is essential for establishing a wireless connection with your target system.

Scan for Devices

Start scanning for available Bluetooth Low Energy devices in your vicinity. The application will automatically search for compatible target devices and display them in a list. Make sure your target device is powered on and in pairing mode.

Establish Connection

Select your target device from the discovered list and establish a secure Bluetooth connection. Once connected, you'll see a confirmation message and can begin communicating with your target system. The connection status will be displayed at the top of the screen.
